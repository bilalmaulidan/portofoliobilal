"Every medium is a new language — and this portfolio is the first conversation."

The Purpose

This digital exhibition brings together five distinct multimedia assignments created as part of a university communications program. Each piece explores a different medium — from data visualization to interactive games — unified by a shared curiosity about how ideas travel through different forms.

The Journey

The creative process was iterative, experimental, and often unexpected. Research led to design decisions. Design decisions revealed gaps in understanding. Gaps led back to research. This cyclical rhythm defined the semester.
